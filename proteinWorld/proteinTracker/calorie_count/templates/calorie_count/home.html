Struggle with remembering your macros for each day? Have no worry with Protein Tracker Here!

The app is very easy to operate:

  1. Click on the 'Calculate Macros' button to lead you to our Macro Calculator.
  2. Enter the food name and amount you want to check and submit it.
  3. Our calculator will give you both the food and your total macros.
  4. For more items, just press 'Go back' and input any other foods!

Disclaimer: All total records are wiped after you exit the session. We acknowledge Edamam in the use of their API for our macro calculator.
