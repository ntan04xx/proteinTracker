{% load static %} Protein Tracker: Your Everyday App for Easy Macro Maintenance

Struggle with remembering your macros for each day? Have no worry with Protein Tracker Here!

To find the nutrient breakdown of a food, just:

  1. Click on the 'Calculate Macros' button to lead you to our Macro Calculator.
  2. Enter the food name and amount you want to check and submit it.
  3. Our calculator will give you both the food and your total macros for the day.
  4. For more items, just press 'Go back' and input any other foods!

As for tracking your daily targets, just:

  1. Click on the 'Track Progress' button to lead you to our Progress Checker.
  2. Enter your username and submit it.
  3. Our checker will show which daily targets you have met and not met and by how much.
  4. To add a food item, just press 'Add Item' and follow the above procedure!
